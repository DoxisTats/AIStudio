Work Visa & Relocation Policy

1. Purpose

This policy establishes the framework for supporting employees who require work visa sponsorship or are relocating internationally as part of their employment with the organisation. It applies to all permanent and fixed-term employees.

2. Visa Sponsorship

The organisation will consider sponsoring work visas for roles that cannot be filled by the local talent pool. Sponsorship requests must be approved by the Head of HR and the relevant department head prior to any application submission.

  • Tier 2 / Skilled Worker visa: standard processing 8–12 weeks
  • EU Blue Card: applicable for senior and specialised roles
  • Intracompany transfer: minimum 12 months prior employment required

3. Relocation Support

Employees relocating at the organisation's request are entitled to a relocation allowance. The amount is determined by the distance of relocation and the employee's grade level.

  • Domestic relocation (>50 km): up to €2,500
  • International relocation: up to €8,000 plus temporary accommodation for 30 days
  • Family relocation supplement: additional €3,000 per dependent

4. Process

All relocation and visa requests must be submitted via the HR portal at least 90 days before the intended start date.
